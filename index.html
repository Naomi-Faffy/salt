Our Story

A Culinary Journey of Passion

Founded by acclaimed Chef Michel Laurent in 2015, Lumière represents the culmination of a lifelong dedication to culinary excellence. Our philosophy centers on transforming the finest seasonal ingredients into unforgettable dining experiences.

Each dish tells a story—of tradition reimagined, of boundaries pushed, of flavors discovered. We invite you to join us on this gastronomic journey where every meal is a celebration of artistry and taste.

Michel Laurent, Executive Chef
