Our Story

A Culinary Journey

Nestled in the heart of Harare, SALT offers an unparalleled dining experience that celebrates the rich flavors of Zimbabwe with contemporary culinary techniques.

Established in 2018, our restaurant has quickly become the city's most sought-after destination for those seeking exceptional cuisine in an elegant setting.

Each dish tells a story of our heritage, reimagined through the creative vision of our award-winning chefs.

The Experience

Elegant Ambiance

Step into a world of refined elegance where every detail has been carefully considered. Our dining room combines contemporary design with subtle nods to Zimbabwe's rich cultural heritage.

Soft lighting, sophisticated décor, and comfortable seating create the perfect backdrop for an unforgettable dining experience.

For special occasions, our private dining room offers an intimate setting for up to 12 guests.

Culinary Artistry

Meet Our Chef

With over 15 years of experience in renowned restaurants across Europe and Africa, our Executive Chef brings a wealth of knowledge and passion to SALT.

Trained in classical techniques and inspired by global flavors, the chef's innovative approach to cuisine has earned numerous accolades and a devoted following.

Each dish reflects a commitment to excellence and a deep respect for ingredients, resulting in a dining experience that delights all the senses.
